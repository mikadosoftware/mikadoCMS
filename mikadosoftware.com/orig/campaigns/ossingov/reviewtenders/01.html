Kent County Council is seeking to obtain expressions of interest in providing a hosted e-recruitment system for use by the Council and associated technical implementation services. The main objectives for the new system will be to:


• Enable the Council to manage the end-to-end recruitment process fully from vacancy requisition through to ‘on-boarding’
of the successful candidate(s)
• Deliver process efficiencies through increased automation and task alerts
• Provide flexibility to meet business requirements through customisable and configurable
workflows, application forms, templates and user permissions
• Improve data quality through validated e-forms
• Reduce duplication of entry through increased manager ‘self-service’ including vacancy
requisition, shortlisting, interview scheduling and scoring
• Improve the effectiveness and efficiency of Priority Connect (Redeployment) and other talent
management processes through the automated competency-based matching and on-system
candidate / talent pools
• Enhance candidate engagement to attract and retain the best talent
• Provide enhanced reporting capability and management information
• Promote opportunities for income-generation by enabling the provision of recruitment services,
by the Council, to customer organisations, including branded access.


Solutions will need to be hosted and fully supported.


Providers are invited to submit an expression of interest and complete a pre-qualification
questionnaire at: www.kentbusinessportal.org.uk.
