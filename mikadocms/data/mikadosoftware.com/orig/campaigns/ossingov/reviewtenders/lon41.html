Total Talent / Employee Performance Management Solution

London Borough of Croydon invites tenders from suppliers to provide a ‘Total Talent Management Solution’ to move the performance management system of the council from a paper based system to an online system of  employee performance management, in support of both workforce management and development and with the capability to balance the diversity of the organisation across all levels through interrogation of the data. Functionality must include:

 

  • Compensation modelling facility based on appraisal /performance outcomes
  • Competency analysis and modelling to identify skills gaps and allow for focussed training either at an individual, unit, group or Council wide level.
  • Competency based redeployment pools reducing redundancy and therefore, costs.
  • Succession planning, utilising  information to develop and manage potential successors for key positions, improving  internal mobility with the potential to save significantly on Interim management costs, cost of search, recruiting and organisational “acclimatisation
  • Full appraisal (including 360o)  correlated to corporate targets and relevant and pertinent development plans, from both the individual and organisation perspective
  • Organisation charting and “what if” analysis
  • Opportunity for the identification of good and ineffectual managers and providing the organisation a transparent view on how to improve performance
  • Robust and integrated reporting capability including ‘dashboard’ deployment for senior management and Human Resource Professionals

The system must be ‘live’ by 31st January 2012.
