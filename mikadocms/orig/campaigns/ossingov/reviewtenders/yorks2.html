Fuel Management System Contract – Supplier Engagement Day, Monday 18th February 2013


Further to the notification that the original bidder day, scheduled for 22nd January, was postponed due to adverse weather.


East Riding of Yorkshire Council will shortly be tendering for the provision of a fuel management system. This contract will include the provision, installation and on-going maintenance of a fuel management system (software) and related equipment, to manage fuel stocks including monitoring levels, controlling access, recharging internal customers and reporting on fuel usage across seven council sites. Solutions are required to integrate with the council’s existing automatic number plate recognition system (ANPR), vehicle tracking and fleet management systems.


The council is holding a supplier engagement day for interested parties on Monday 18th February 2013 at the Goole Council Chambers, Church Street, Town Centre, Goole, DN14 5BQ. All suppliers who may be interested in this opportunity are invited to attend.


A location map for Goole Council Chambers can be found attached to this advert. Parking information for Goole is below*.


The supplier engagement day will be an opportunity for suppliers to find out details about the council’s requirement and to ask questions about our requirement and the procurement process. It will also be a chance to meet the key stakeholders involved in the process, and representatives from the companies with whose systems your solution will need to integrate.


Representatives present on the day will include individuals from:



  • The council’s Fleet, ICT and Procurement teams

  • The council’s automatic number plate recognition system (ANPR) provider

  • The council’s vehicle tracking system provider

  • The council’s fleet management system provider (TBC)


Please see the provisional programme attached to this advert for an outline of the day. If you are interested in tendering for this opportunity then we strongly recommend that you attend this day.


If you wish to attend the this event then please email Carl Gillyon, Fleet Services Manager (carl.gillyon@eastriding.gov.uk) and Ellie Cope, Senior Procurement Officer (ellie.cope@eastriding.gov.uk) to confirm your attendance. Please confirm your company name and the names and numbers of attendees from your organisation.


We would be grateful if you could confirm your attendance no later than 5pm Thursday 14th February 2013.  If you confirmed attendance at the previous bidder day then please contatc us to re-confirm for the new date.


Please note that you can also select the “Express Interest” button to express your general interest on this contract.


Please note that this tender opportunity has not yet been advertised and that this is a notification about the supplier engagement day only – the tender opportunity will be advertised through ERCF after the supplier engagement day has taken place.
