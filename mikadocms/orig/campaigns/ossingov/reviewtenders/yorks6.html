East Riding of Yorkshire Council is seeking tenders from suitably qualified and experienced economic operators to assist in the redesign of the Council responsive housing repairs processes through the use of mobile technology. 


The solution must connect seamlessly with our current housing management software (Capita Housing Academy) and include job management, scheduling and mobile working elements to enable efficiencies and cost savings to be achieved. 


Further information on the Council's requirements is contained within the tender documentation for download.
